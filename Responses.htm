How do I cancel my line ?

To cancel your line, kindly contact the concerned team on the line 17882855 and they will assist you with the cancellation. Working hours: Sanday to Thursday (From 9AM to 5PM).

How to downgrade My mobile package (Change my package to a lower package)?

To downgrade your mobile package, kindly send an email to this address ecc@btc.com.bh and you will be contacted by the concerned team. Make sure to attach the required documents: Valid CR Copy, Authorized CPR Copy, Signed and stamped Application Form (Mobile-Postpaid-Application-Form.pdf (batelco.com)).please note that according to the terms and conditions, downgrading to a lower package before your contract ends may require a cancellation fee. This fee is calculated as the rental amount multiplied by the number of remaining months, in addition to any outstanding device or any nice number agreement penalties. Downgrade fee: BD5.5.

How to change ownership?

To change the ownership, kindly send an email to this address ecc@btc.com.bh and you will be contacted by the concerned team. Make sure to attach the required documents: Valid CR Copies, Authorized CPR Copy for both partners, Signed and stamped Application Form. Mobile form (Mobile-Postpaid-Application-Form.pdf (batelco.com). Fixed line form ( batelco.com/pdf/batelco-fixed-line-isdn-form.pdf). Changing ownership fee: BD5.5.

 How do I change the location of my line (shift line)?

To change the Location of your line (External Removal or Internal removal), kindly send an email to this address ecc@btc.com.bh and you will be contacted by the concerned team. Make sure to attach the required documents: Valid CR Copy Authorized CPR Copy , Signed and stamped Application Form ( batelco.com/pdf/batelco-fixed-line-isdn-form.pdf). Changing ownership fee: BD22 per line.

How to Activate Data add-ons & Data Roaming?

To activate Data roaming add-ons Login to your e-Services Account  ( https://www.e.batelco.com/eservices/Login ), Click on My Services from the top menu, Select the desired number from the Number Selector, Go to Get Add-ons Card, Expand the required category, Switch ON the Toggle of the required Add-on, Click on Confirm. NOTE:  For packages, countries and operators details, click on the link (https://batelco.com/mobile/roaming/).

 

Red Box: To connect your Batelco Redbox, kindly follow the demonstration video in the link  (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YJnEEf5MTCk ).
